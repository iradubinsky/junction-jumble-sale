FAQ
How does it work?
A neighbourhood-wide yard sale where everyone hosts a yard sale on the same day. Everyone selling independently on their front lawns. Inspired by the Great Glebe Garage Sale in Ottawa.
When and where is the sale?
Saturday May 23rd, 2026 from 8:30AM to 2:30PM across the Junction neighbourhood. As of now we have confirmations from lots of folkso on Quebec, Clendenan, Laws, Evelyn, Fairview, Gilmour, St. John's and more. Everyone is welcome to participate.
What if it rains?
The rain date is Saturday May 30th. Check the Facebook event for updates closer to the date.
How do I register my house?
Click the "Register Your House" button above and fill out the short form. Your address will appear on the map automatically. If you do not want to register, that is totally OK.
Is there a fee to participate?
Nope — it's completely free to register and participate. Just set up on your lawn or driveway and you're good to go.
What can I sell?
Anything goes — clothing, furniture, toys, books, kitchenware, electronics, plants, your own crafts or art, even homemade baked goods.
Do I need to be home all day?
You can participate for as long or as little as you like.
How will people know about the event?
Volunteers are promoting the event on social media, garage sale listings and with signs around the neighborhood.
How can I contact the organizers?
Check out the Facebook event and leave us a comment there.
